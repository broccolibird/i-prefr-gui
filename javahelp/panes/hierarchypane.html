What is a Role Hierarchy?

Add Roles to Hierarchy

In order to add a Role to the Hierarchy, start by verifying that the graph is in Editing mode. Then, select the Role to be added from the Role drop-down menu. Click on the graph view to place the Role in the Hierarchy.


Create a Role Hierarchy

After adding two or more Roles to the graph, you can start adding Role relationships. While still in Editing mode, click on a Role and drag the cursor to a subordinate Role. Continue adding Roles and relationships until you have finished the Role Hierarchy.




Annotations

Annotations for personal use can be added to Roles on the graph while in Annotating mode.


Controls

Role

Select a Role from the drop-down box to add to the Hierarchy graph


Mode

EDITING Add Roles to the Hierarchy graph and create links between Roles to show relationships.
TRANSFORMING Click and drag the entire viewing window to focus on different areas.
PICKING Click a Role and drag it to the desired location within the graph view.
ANNOTATING Right click on a Role in order to add an informational annotation to it on the graph.

Zoom

Use the zoom controls to increase (+) or decrease (-) the size of the graph.
